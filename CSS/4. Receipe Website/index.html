Grilled Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless chicken breast
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 2 tbsp Greek yogurt (plain)
  • 1/4 sliced tomato
  • Salt, pepper, chili flakes
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  2. Grill it in olive oil until golden and cooked through.
  3. Spread Greek yogurt on the tortilla.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and grilled chicken slices.
  5. Wrap tightly and serve.

Greek Yogurt Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 scoop protein powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass.
  3. Top with chia seeds or oats if desired.
  4. Serve chilled.
